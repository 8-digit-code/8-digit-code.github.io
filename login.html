Login to Disney+



Disney+ offers a world of entertainment with its vast library of content from Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar, and National Geographic. If you're ready to start streaming, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you log in and activate Disney+ using Disneyplus.com/begin.

How to Create a Disney Plus Account?

  1. Open your browser and go to disneyplus.com/begin.
  2. You’ll see a “Sign Up Now” button on the homepage. Click on it to begin the registration process.
  3. Provide a valid email address that you’ll use to log in to your Disney+ account. Click “Continue.”
  4. Create a strong password for your account. Ensure it’s something secure that you’ll remember.
  5. Select the subscription plan that best suits your needs. Disney+ offers various plans, including monthly and annual options.
  6. Provide your payment details to complete the sign-up process.
  7. Once your payment is processed, you’re all set! Log in with your email and password to start enjoying Disney+ content.

How to Log In to Your Disney Plus Account?

  1. Open the Disney+ website on your browser or launch the app on your device.
  2. Look for the “Log In” button, usually located at the top right corner of the homepage.
  3. Provide the email address you used to create your Disney+ account.
  4. Type in your password.
  5. Hit the “Log In” button to access your account.

Frequently Asked Questions about Logging in to Disney+ via Disneyplus.com/begin

Is disneyplus.com/begin necessary for each device?

Yes, disneyplus.com/begin is required to activate Disney+ on each new device you want to use. After the initial activation, however, the device remains linked to your account.


Can I log into Disney+ on multiple devices?

Yes, Disney+ allows simultaneous streaming on multiple devices, depending on your subscription plan. Use disneyplus.com/begin to activate each device individually.


Do I need to re-enter my code if I log out?

Once activated, your device will remain logged in to your account unless you manually log out or reset the app.
