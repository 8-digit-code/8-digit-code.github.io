Stream DisneyPlus



Discover easy steps to watch Disney+ on your TV. Activate and start streaming Disney+ via Disneyplus.com/begin on smart TVs, streaming devices, and more.

Watching Disney+ on your TV can transform your living room into a home theater, bringing a vast library of Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar, and National Geographic content to the big screen. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started using Disneyplus.com/begin:

How to Watch Disney+ on Various TV Setups?


Watching Disney+ on a Smart TV


  • Open your TV’s app store and search for “Disney+.” Download and install the app.
  • Once installed, open the app and log in to your Disney+ account. A unique activation code will appear on the screen.
  • On a different device (smartphone or computer), open a browser and go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Enter the code from your TV screen and click Continue. The app should refresh, and you’ll be ready to start streaming.

Using Streaming Devices (Roku, Amazon Fire Stick, Chromecast)


  • Open the app store on your streaming device, search for “Disney+,” and install the app.
  • Open Disney+ and log in, which will prompt an activation code on your screen.
  • On a mobile device or computer, visit Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Input the activation code from your streaming device screen and select Continue. Disney+ will now be active on your streaming device.

Watching Disney+ on Gaming Consoles (PlayStation and Xbox)


  • Go to the app store on your console (PlayStation Store or Microsoft Store) and search for Disney+. Download and install the app.
  • Open the Disney+ app, log in, and note the activation code displayed on the screen.
  • Use a secondary device to go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Input the code displayed on your console screen, confirm, and your console will link to your Disney+ account.

Casting Disney+ to Your TV (for Chromecast Users)


  • Download the Disney+ app on your smartphone or tablet if you haven’t already.
  • Ensure your phone and Chromecast are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
  • Open the Disney+ app, select a title to play, and tap the Cast icon in the app. Choose your Chromecast device, and the content will play on your TV screen.
