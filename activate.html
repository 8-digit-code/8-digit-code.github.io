Activate DisneyPlus



Activating Disney+ via disneyplus.com/begin is a simple process that lets you enjoy the best of Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, and Pixar on all your devices. Follow these steps, troubleshoot any issues with ease, and get ready to stream your favorites wherever you go. Happy streaming!

Check Compatibility for Disney+

Disney+ is compatible with various devices, making it easy for you to stream on whichever device you prefer. Compatible devices include:

  • Smart TVs: Samsung, LG, Sony, and more
  • Streaming Devices: Roku, Amazon Fire Stick, and Chromecast
  • Game Consoles: PlayStation and Xbox
  • Mobile Devices: iOS and Android smartphones or tablets
  • Web Browsers: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge

How to Activate Disney+ on Different Devices via Disneyplus.com/begin?

Smart TV

  • Download and install the Disney+ app from your TV’s app store.
  • Open the app and select "Log In." An 8-digit code will appear on your screen.
  • On a computer or mobile browser, go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Input the 8-digit code displayed on your TV screen.
  • Enter your Disney+ account email and password.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the activation process. Your TV will refresh and display the Disney+ home screen.

Streaming Devices (Roku, Amazon Fire Stick, Apple TV, Chromecast)

  • Download and install the Disney+ app from the device’s app store.
  • Launch the Disney+ app and select "Log In."
  • An activation code will appear on your screen.
  • On a browser, go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Type in the 8-digit code displayed on your device.
  • Enter your Disney+ account credentials.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the activation process.

Gaming Consoles (PlayStation, Xbox)

  • Go to the console’s app store and download the Disney+ app.
  • Open the app and select "Log In." An activation code will be displayed.
  • Use a computer or mobile browser to go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Input the code from your gaming console’s screen.
  • Enter your Disney+ email and password.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the setup.

Mobile Devices (Smartphones, Tablets)

  • Install the Disney+ app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Launch the app and enter your Disney+ credentials to log in.
  • Mobile devices typically do not require an activation code. Once logged in, you can start streaming immediately.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Expired Code: Activation codes have a time limit. If your code expires, restart the process to get a new one.
  • Incorrect Code Entry: Ensure you enter the code exactly as it appears on your device screen.
  • Device Compatibility: Check that your device is compatible with Disney+. Visit the Disney+ support page for a list of supported devices.
  • Internet Connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the process.
