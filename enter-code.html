DisneyPlus Enter 8 Digit Code



The Disney+ 8-digit code is an activation code you get when you log in to Disney+ on a new device. It appears on your screen and is used to link your device to your Disney+ account. You enter this code on Disneyplus.com/begin to complete the activation. It’s a simple way to ensure your device is securely connected to your account.

Why you need an Activation Code?

  • Connect Devices: It links your Disney Plus account to your TV or streaming device.
  • Start Streaming: Once activated, you can watch Disney Plus on your big screen.
  • Easy Setup: The code makes the setup process simple and quick.

Where to find 8 digit Disney+ Plus code?

You can find the 8-digit Disney+ code when you log in to your Disney+ account on a new device:

  • Launch the Disney+ app on your smart TV, streaming device, or mobile device.
  • Enter your Disney+ account credentials (email and password).
  • An 8-digit activation code will appear on your screen.

Where do I enter my 8 digit Disney+ Plus code?

  • On your computer, tablet, or smartphone, go to Disneyplus.com/begin.
  • Type in the 8-digit code displayed on your TV or device screen.
  • Log in to your Disney+ account using your email and password.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the activation process.

Common Problems While Entering Disney Plus Activation Code

  • Code Expired: The activation code has a time limit. If you don't enter it within the given timeframe, it will expire. To fix this, simply restart the process to get a new code.
  • Incorrect Code Entry: Ensure you enter the code exactly as it appears, without spaces or extra characters.
  • Device Compatibility: Make sure your device is compatible with Disney+. Check the Disney+ support page for a list of supported devices.
  • Internet Connection Issues: A weak or unstable internet connection can cause problems. Ensure your connection is stable before attempting activation.
  • App Issues: Sometimes, uninstalling and reinstalling the Disney+ app can resolve activation problems.
  • Server Issues: Occasionally, Disney+ servers might be down. Check Disney+ service status online to see if there are any known issues.
  • Browser Cache: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try entering the code again.
  • Login Credentials: Double-check that you're using the correct Disney+ account credentials.
